Last.fm Song Recommendation Tool

This tool recommends songs that you have never scrobbled.

           

The first and second algorithm are identical when using loved tracks.

Choosing a larger number of songs to use makes your recommendations more accurate, but uses much more CPU. Leaving it at 20 is usually sufficient.

Calculation Algorithm

Tracks sampled from your library.

Recommendations are calculated based on similarity to tracks in your library.

Recommendations are calculated based on which tracks are similar to the most played tracks in your library.

Recommendations are calculated based on what your closest neighbours listen to.

Recommendations are calculated based on what your friends listen to.

A mix of the above.
